#### 1♣-1♦-1NT-2♣-2♥

2♠
: no 5♥ or 4♠ (sbsq. 2NT or 3m extras, 3NT w/o extras)

2NT
: 4♥ 4♠ extras

3NT
: 4♥ 4♠ w/o extras

#### 1♣-1♥-1NT-2♣-2♥

2♠
: 0-3♥ 4♠(sbsq. 2NT or 3m extras, 3NT w/o extras)

2NT
: 0-3♥ 5♠ w/extras

3NT
: 0-3♥ 5♠ w/o extras

#### 1♣-1♦-1♥-2♦-2♥

2♠
: w/o 4♠, just trf to NT

2NT
: 4♠ extras

3NT
: 4♠ no extras

#### 1♣-1♥-1♠-2♦-2♥

2♠
: w/o 5♠, just trf to NT

2NT
: 5♠ extras

3NT
: 5♠ no extras
